Scoring Dimensions

Strategic Fit (20%)

Whether continuous site performance, SEO, and conversion optimisation are priorities that tie to measurable business goals.

Technical Fit (20%)

Whether the site runs on Edge Delivery Services or AEM (best fit) and how optimisation is handled today.

Functional Coverage (20%)

Overlap with Sites Optimizer capabilities — performance, Core Web Vitals, SEO, accessibility, and conversion insights.

Commercial Viability (15%)

Site scale and traffic, and whether the optimisation value justifies the add-on.

Competitive Position (15%)

Incumbent SEO/performance tools (BrightEdge, Conductor, Botify, Lumar) and Adobe's native EDS advantage.

Delivery Risk (10%)

Effort to act on recommendations and integrate optimisation into the team's workflow.

Deployment Models

SaaS add-on for Edge Delivery Services

Best fit — plugs directly into EDS sites for automated, continuous optimisation.

AEM Sites attach

Works with AEM Sites content to surface optimisation opportunities.

Standalone monitoring

Can monitor a site to surface insights even before deeper integration.

Conversion & traffic insights

Key Signals

Underperforming site

Failing Core Web Vitals, slow LCP, or declining rankings — direct Sites Optimizer value.

Already on EDS or AEM

Existing Adobe site investment — a natural, low-friction attach.

SEO / organic traffic priority

Organic is a major channel and rankings matter — SEO insights.

Accessibility / WCAG mandate

Legal or policy accessibility requirements — accessibility scanning.

Conversion optimisation goals

An active CRO program or revenue-per-visit focus.

Large or fast-changing site

Many pages or frequent publishing — continuous monitoring beats manual audits.

Lean dev / marketing team

Wants self-service optimisation without heavy engineering — guided fixes.

Marketing wants autonomy

Reduce reliance on developers for performance and SEO work.

Red Flags

Not on AEM / EDS

Limited integration depth — qualify the fit carefully.

No traffic or SEO concern

Low-traffic or internal site with no optimisation driver.

Mature optimisation stack

Already invested in tooling they are satisfied with — displacement is hard.

Tiny site

Too few pages to justify continuous optimisation.

Competitive Alternatives

BrightEdge / Conductor

Enterprise SEO platforms. Sites Optimizer wins on native EDS integration and automated remediation.

Botify / Lumar

Technical SEO crawlers. Sites Optimizer wins on built-in fixes within the Adobe stack.

Lighthouse / PageSpeed

Free performance audits. Sites Optimizer wins on continuous monitoring and guided action.

Siteimprove

Accessibility and QA. Sites Optimizer wins as part of an integrated Adobe experience stack.
